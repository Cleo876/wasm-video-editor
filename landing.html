VISUAL EFFECTS

Node‑Based Compositing

Build professional compositing chains directly on your clips. Connect nodes for colour grading, glows, vignettes, masks, and merges — and watch the result update in real time. The graph overlays on top of your original media, so you can always toggle back.

Every node type is colour‑coded — yellow for image, blue for mask, green for value — making complex graphs readable at a glance. Import custom .js nodes from the community, or write your own to create effects no other editor can match.

DYNAMIC MOVEMENT

Transitions that Flow

Custom transitions created as tiny .js modules. Define your animation on a canvas, set the timing and alignment, and the engine handles the rest — including seamless half‑and‑half cross‑fades where the animation continues smoothly across the cut.

Every transition can auto‑reverse, respect a maximum duration, and export the exact same look to FFmpeg for your final render. The result is motion that feels intentional and cinematic, never cheap.
